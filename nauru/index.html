Nauru is an island nation located in the Micronesian archipelago of the South Pacific, where invasion, independence and resources are the three key words for the country. Using Nauru's phosphate resources as a starting point, the project researched the position of the frigate bird in Nauru and the history of Nauru to create a Helvetiru typeface and web design for the country. The visuals for the project were created by carving and re-topping wooden panels. After the invasion and independence Nauru was briefly rich because of its phosphate resources. Through this project it is hoped that more people will learn about Nauru, recognise the value of non-renewable resources and protect them.
