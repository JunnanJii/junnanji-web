Latitude and Longitude was completed in a feedback session in the first semester of third year, exploring the visualisation of latitude and longitude lines from the property of latitude and longitude. The project created a series of posters based on the latitude and longitude lines of fixed objects and the patterns of latitude and longitude lines created by the movement of people. Initially I researched the latitude and longitude of a person's passage through the day and visualised them, and after different attempts I decided to create the posters using image manipulation and graphic production. I recorded the graphs and lines created by the latitude and longitude lines on the beach walk, the latitude and longitude of fishing boats and the latitude and longitude of a student's day to create four posters.
